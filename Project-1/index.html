About Us

Hello traveler ! We welcome you to our humble shop . Our shop offers a selection of enchanting tattoos designed by our artists . These skilled artist can help you craft custom tattoos or recreate tattoos of your desires . We also provide guidance through consultations and aftercare instructions to preserve your divine markings . Let these ornaments serve as a wonderous narrator of your story or simply an expression of your whimsy . Whether it's your first venture into this artistic world or you're a seasonned traveler, we hope you enjoy your visit here .


We started this shop as we believe that art is an amazing way to express yourself . . . and we love the galaxy / deity theme . Whether it's with words, symbols or illustrations, we believe all are magnificent manners to show your interests and tell your tales . Here at Ink Galaxy, we'll try our absolute best to give you a fabulous experience and a bit of magic dust to go no matter the location you choose .
